### Column Descriptions:
1. **Req. ID**: Unique identifier for tracking throughout the project lifecycle.
2. **Category**: Classification (Functional, Non-Functional, Technical, or Compliance).
3. **Source**: Origin of the requirement (Stakeholders, Students, or Legal standards).
4. **WBS Ref**: Reference to the Work Breakdown Structure package where development occurs.
5. **Test Case ID**: The specific test script used by the QA Team to verify completion.
6. **Status**: Current state of the requirement (Open, In-Progress, Verified, or Failed).
